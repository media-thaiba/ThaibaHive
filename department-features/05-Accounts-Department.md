# ThaibaHive — Features for Accounts Department

Dear Accounts Department Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Financial Accounts Dashboard** — Income/expense tracking per institution; transaction ledger; summary dashboards with filters by date, category, institution
2. **Expense Claims** — Staff bill submissions with photo upload; approval workflow; reimbursement tracking and history
3. **Purchase Requests** — Multi-stage approval pipeline (Staff → HOD → **Accounts** → Purchase); audit trail for each request
4. **Asset Management (Financial)** — Asset registry with purchase value, depreciation tracking; QR-tagged lifecycle management
5. **Approval Center** — Unified inbox for all pending Accounts approvals (purchases, expenses)
6. **Leave Balances** — View leave balances per staff member; leave-linked salary processing reference
7. **Staff Directory** — Access staff bank details and payment information (secured)
8. **Staff Onboarding/Offboarding Checklists** — Financial clearance tracking during exits
9. **Announcements** — Finance-related announcements to departments/institutions
10. **Events Calendar** — Financial deadlines, audit dates, payment schedules
11. **Circulars** — Store financial policies, tax guidelines, audit reports
12. **Export & Compliance** — Financial data export for audits and tax filing (CSV/Excel)
13. **Vehicle Logs** — Fuel expense and distance logs for fleet cost tracking
14. **Canteen Reports** — Meal consumption data for canteen subsidy calculations
15. **Audit Log** — Complete action trail for financial transactions

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for the Accounts Department?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., payroll integration, budget planning, GST/tax filing modules)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
