# ThaibaHive — Features for Vehicle Department

Dear Vehicle Department Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Vehicle Management (Fleet Registry)** — Complete vehicle database (make, model, registration, insurance, fitness, permit expiry); QR-tagged
2. **Vehicle Bookings (Trip Requests)** — Staff request vehicles for trips; driver assignment; approval workflow; calendar avoids double-booking
3. **Vehicle Logs** — Fuel consumption, distance/odometer, trip records per vehicle
4. **Driver Attendance** — Track driver check-in/out and duty hours
5. **Staff Availability** — Live driver availability status
6. **Maintenance Requests** — Report vehicle issues via Help Desk; service/repair history tracking
7. **Asset Service History** — Log vehicle servicing, repairs, and part replacements
8. **Approval Center** — Approve/reject trip requests and fuel expenses
9. **Expense Claims** — Submit fuel bills, toll charges, and maintenance expenses
10. **Purchase Requests** — Request vehicle parts, tyres, or new vehicle procurement
11. **Announcements** — Transport schedule changes and route updates
12. **Staff Directory** — Contact details for drivers and transport coordinators
13. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day transport activity summaries
14. **Task Management** — Vehicle repair and maintenance task tracking

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for the Vehicle Department?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., route planning, GPS tracking, fuel card management)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
