# ThaibaHive — Features for Moral Academy

Dear Moral Academy Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Staff Directory** — Searchable directory of Moral Academy staff across campuses
2. **Attendance Management** — Staff check-in/out; department-wise attendance reports
3. **Announcements** — Broadcast moral education updates, program notices, and event info
4. **Events Calendar** — Moral education programs, workshops, and seminars
5. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Assign and track program preparation tasks
6. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day activity summaries
7. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Collect feedback on moral education initiatives
8. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize outstanding moral instructors and contributions
9. **Circulars** — Store curriculum materials, lesson plans, and program guides
10. **Staff Availability** — Live presence status for coordination
11. **Leave Management** — Apply and approve leave digitally
12. **Performance Reviews** — Instructor evaluations and goal tracking
13. **Expense Claims** — Submit program-related expenses
14. **Purchase Requests** — Request teaching materials and supplies

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for Moral Academy?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., curriculum planner, student moral assessment, event attendance tracking for students)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
