# ThaibaHive — Features for Feed The Needy (FTN)

Dear FTN Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Event Management** — Plan and publish food distribution events; track RSVPs and volunteer participation
2. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Assign and track volunteer tasks for each drive/event
3. **Announcements** — Broadcast drive schedules, volunteer calls, and donation needs to specific groups
4. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Coordinate volunteer availability and collect feedback on events
5. **Daily Reports** — End-of-drive reports with distribution numbers, location, and resource usage
6. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize volunteers and donors with kudos and appreciation posts
7. **Staff Directory** — Find volunteers and coordinators across institutions
8. **Attendance** — Track volunteer check-in at distribution sites
9. **Expense Claims** — Submit bills for food purchases, transportation, and supplies
10. **Purchase Requests** — Request food supplies, packaging materials, and transport
11. **Events Calendar** — View all FTN activities alongside institution calendar
12. **Circulars** — Store FTN reports, impact stories, and partnership documents
13. **Staff Availability** — Volunteer availability status for drive planning
14. **Help Desk** — Report logistical issues during drives

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for FTN?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., donation tracking, beneficiary registry, inventory management for food supplies)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
