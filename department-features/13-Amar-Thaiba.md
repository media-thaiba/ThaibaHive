# ThaibaHive — Features for Amar Thaiba

Dear Amar Thaiba Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Announcements** — Publish magazine issues, content updates, and editorial announcements
2. **Events Calendar** — Plan publication launches, writing workshops, and team meetings
3. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Content workflow: assignments, writing, editing, layout, publishing
4. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Collect article ideas and reader preferences
5. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize writers, photographers, and contributors
6. **Daily Reports** — Content production tracking and issue progress
7. **Staff Directory** — Contact contributors and team members
8. **Attendance** — Track team attendance and availability
9. **Circulars** — Store editorial policies, style guides, and published archives
10. **Expense Claims** — Content production and printing expenses
11. **Purchase Requests** — Request publishing software, printing services, or equipment
12. **Staff Availability** — Contributor availability management

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for Amar Thaiba?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., digital publishing workflow, photography archive, distribution tracking)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
