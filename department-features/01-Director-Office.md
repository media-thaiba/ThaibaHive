# ThaibaHive — Features for Director Office

Dear Director Office Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Executive Dashboard** — Real-time overview of attendance, pending approvals, latecomers, absentees, and institution-wide metrics at a glance
2. **Staff Directory** — Full searchable directory of all employees across all 23+ institutions with role, department, and institution filters
3. **Attendance Overview** — Institution-wide and department-wise attendance reports with late/early/absent tracking
4. **Leave Management (Final Approvals)** — Top-level oversight of leave requests; approve/reject at final stage
5. **Approval Center** — Unified inbox to review and act on all pending approvals (leaves, purchases, expenses, IT requests, bookings)
6. **Task Oversight** — View all tasks across departments; track progress on strategic initiatives
7. **Daily Reports** — Institution-wide report submission compliance and summary views
8. **Performance Reviews** — Quarterly appraisal cycles; review manager-submitted ratings and goals
9. **Org Structure Management** — Add/edit institutions, departments, sub-departments, and shift definitions
10. **Announcements (Broadcast)** — Send priority announcements to all institutions, specific departments, or roles
11. **Events Calendar** — Institution-wide event calendar with RSVP monitoring
12. **Circulars & Document Repository** — Upload and distribute policies, notices, and official documents
13. **Financial Overview** — Income/expense summary per institution; transaction ledger
14. **Audit Log** — Complete action trail for compliance and security review
15. **Staff Recognition** — View and post kudos, Employee of the Month, birthday/work anniversary reminders
16. **Grievance Dashboard** — Anonymous and named feedback from staff; response workflow
17. **Export & Compliance** — Data export for regulatory reporting (CSV/Excel)
18. **Staff Timeline** — Chronological activity feed per employee

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for the Director Office?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
