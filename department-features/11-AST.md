# ThaibaHive — Features for Al Siddiqia Trust (AST)

Dear AST Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Purchase Requests** — Multi-stage approval for trust-related procurement; audit trail
2. **Expense Claims** — Submit and track trust-related expenses with photo upload
3. **Financial Overview** — Income/expense tracking for trust funds; transaction ledger
4. **Announcements** — Broadcast trust updates and initiatives
5. **Events Calendar** — Trust events, meetings, and deadlines
6. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Track trust project tasks
7. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day trust activity summaries
8. **Staff Directory** — Contact details for trustees and trust staff
9. **Attendance** — Track trust staff attendance
10. **Staff Availability** — Live presence status
11. **Circulars** — Store trust deeds, legal documents, and meeting minutes
12. **Approval Center** — Unified inbox for trust-related approvals
13. **Asset Management** — Track trust-owned properties and assets
14. **Grievances** — Feedback and suggestions for trust administration
15. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize contributors to trust initiatives

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for AST?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., beneficiary management, waqf property tracking, donation collection)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
