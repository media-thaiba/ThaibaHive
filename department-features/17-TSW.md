# ThaibaHive — Features for Thaiba Sweet Water (TSW)

Dear TSW Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Expense Claims** — Submit bills for water supply operations; approval workflow; reimbursement tracking
2. **Purchase Requests** — Order water treatment supplies, equipment, and maintenance parts
3. **Attendance Management** — Track TSW staff check-in/out and duty hours
4. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Assign and track water supply, maintenance, and delivery tasks
5. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day production and distribution summaries
6. **Staff Directory** — Contact details for TSW team members
7. **Staff Availability** — Live presence status for shift coordination
8. **Announcements** — Notify about supply schedules, maintenance shutdowns, or water quality updates
9. **Asset Management** — Track water treatment equipment, pumps, tanks; QR-tagged service history
10. **Asset Service History** — Log repairs and servicing of water equipment
11. **Leave Management** — Apply and approve leave digitally
12. **Help Desk** — Report equipment breakdowns or supply issues
13. **Approval Center** — Approve/reject purchase and expense requests
14. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize team contributions

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for TSW?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., water quality testing logs, distribution route planning, customer/vendor management)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
