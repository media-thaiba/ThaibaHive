# ThaibaHive — Features for Candle Of Hope International (COHI)

Dear COHI Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Event Management** — Plan and publish charity events, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns; RSVP tracking
2. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Assign and track volunteer and team tasks
3. **Announcements** — Broadcast campaign calls, event schedules, and impact updates
4. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Coordinate volunteers and collect feedback on initiatives
5. **Daily Reports** — End-of-campaign reports with impact metrics and resource usage
6. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize contributors and volunteers
7. **Staff Directory** — Find volunteers and coordinators
8. **Attendance** — Track team/volunteer presence at events
9. **Expense Claims** — Submit campaign-related expenses
10. **Purchase Requests** — Request materials, supplies, and logistics
11. **Events Calendar** — View COHI activities alongside institution calendar
12. **Circulars** — Store campaign reports, impact stories, and partnership documents
13. **Staff Availability** — Volunteer availability for event planning
14. **Grievances** — Community feedback and suggestions

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for COHI?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., donor management, sponsorship tracking, impact report generation)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
