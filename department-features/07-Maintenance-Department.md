# ThaibaHive — Features for Maintenance Department

Dear Maintenance Department Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Help Desk (Facility Tickets)** — Receive and manage facility maintenance requests; ticket creation, assignment, priority levels, and comment threads
2. **Asset Management** — Track campus assets (buildings, fixtures, equipment); QR-tagged with purchase/warranty/service history
3. **Asset Service History** — Log all repairs, servicing, and part replacements per asset
4. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Maintenance task planning with To Do, In Progress, Review, Completed; priorities and due dates
5. **Purchase Requests** — Order maintenance supplies, tools, and spare parts with multi-stage approval
6. **Expense Claims** — Submit bills for maintenance materials and services
7. **Resource Bookings** — Book tools, equipment, or work areas
8. **Staff Attendance** — Track maintenance staff check-in/out with shift configuration
9. **Staff Directory** — Contact details for maintenance team and supervisors
10. **Announcements** — Notify about facility closures, scheduled maintenance, water/power interruptions
11. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day maintenance activity summaries
12. **Staff Availability** — Live presence status for team coordination
13. **Visitor Management** — Coordinate with visitor entries for external contractors/vendors

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for the Maintenance Department?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., preventive maintenance scheduling, inventory management for spare parts, work order generation)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
