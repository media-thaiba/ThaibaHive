# ThaibaHive — Features for Central Canteen

Dear Central Canteen Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Meal Notifications / Canteen Management** — Staff can notify skip/guest additions per meal (breakfast, lunch, dinner); headcount summary for meal preparation planning
2. **Attendance Management** — Track canteen staff check-in/out and shift hours
3. **Purchase Requests** — Order food supplies, ingredients, and kitchen equipment with approval workflow
4. **Expense Claims** — Submit grocery bills, equipment repairs, and operational expenses
5. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Assign kitchen prep tasks, cleaning schedules, and inventory tasks
6. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day meal counts, wastage, and supply usage summaries
7. **Staff Directory** — Contact canteen team members
8. **Staff Availability** — Live presence status for shift coordination
9. **Announcements** — Menu changes, holiday closures, and special meal notices
10. **Asset Management** — Track kitchen equipment (stoves, fridges, utensils); QR-tagged service history
11. **Asset Service History** — Log repairs and maintenance of kitchen equipment
12. **Leave Management** — Apply and approve leave digitally
13. **Help Desk** — Report kitchen equipment breakdowns or facility issues
14. **Approval Center** — Approve/reject purchase and expense requests
15. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize team contributions

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for Central Canteen?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., menu planning calendar, inventory management, vendor/supplier management, dietary preference tracking)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
