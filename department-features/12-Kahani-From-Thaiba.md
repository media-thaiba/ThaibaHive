# ThaibaHive — Features for Kahani From Thaiba

Dear Kahani From Thaiba Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Announcements** — Publish content releases, story calls, and editorial updates
2. **Events Calendar** — Plan and publish storytelling events, workshops, and meetups
3. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Manage content pipeline: ideation, writing, editing, publishing
4. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Collect story ideas and reader feedback
5. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize contributors, writers, and featured storytellers
6. **Daily Reports** — Content production tracking and weekly summaries
7. **Staff Directory** — Contact contributors and team members
8. **Attendance** — Track team attendance and availability
9. **Circulars** — Store editorial guidelines, style guides, and published works
10. **Expense Claims** — Submit content production expenses
11. **Purchase Requests** — Request publishing tools, equipment, or event supplies
12. **Availability** — Freelancer/contributor availability for assignments

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for Kahani From Thaiba?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., content calendar, author database, submission portal)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
