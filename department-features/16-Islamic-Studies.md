# ThaibaHive — Features for Islamic Studies Department

Dear Islamic Studies Department Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Circulars & Document Repository** — Store Quranic resources, Hadith references, curriculum materials, and religious guidelines
2. **Announcements** — Broadcast prayer timings, Islamic event notices, and program announcements
3. **Events Calendar** — Islamic calendar events, Ramadan schedule, Eid programs, lectures, and seminars
4. **Staff Directory** — Contact Islamic studies teachers and scholars across institutions
5. **Staff Recognition** — Recognize outstanding teachers and contributions
6. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Collect feedback on Islamic programs and initiatives
7. **Task Management (Kanban)** — Plan and track curriculum development and event tasks
8. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day activity summaries
9. **Attendance** — Track Islamic studies staff attendance
10. **Staff Availability** — Live presence status
11. **Leave Management** — Apply and approve leave digitally
12. **Expense Claims** — Submit program and event expenses
13. **Purchase Requests** — Request Islamic books, materials, and supplies

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for the Islamic Studies Department?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., Quran memorization tracking, Islamic dates converter, student religious activity tracking)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
