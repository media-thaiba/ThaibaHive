# ThaibaHive — Features for Joint Director Office

Dear Joint Director / HR Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Staff Directory & HR Profiles** — Full employee profiles with qualifications, certificates, Aadhaar, PAN, bank details, emergency contacts, contract dates, teaching subjects
2. **Staff Management** — Add/edit employee records; assign to departments, institutions, and shifts
3. **Attendance Management** — View, edit, and manage attendance logs; late/early detection; manual entry
4. **Leave Management** — Full apply→approve workflow; leave type configuration; balance tracking per staff per year; department-wise overview
5. **Approval Center** — Unified inbox for all approvals; approval delegation (assign backup approvers)
6. **Task Management** — Create, assign, and track tasks across departments; Kanban view (To Do, In Progress, Review, Completed)
7. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day report submission tracking; compliance monitoring
8. **Checklists (Onboarding/Offboarding)** — Template-based checklists for new joiners and exits; per-staff task tracking
9. **Performance Reviews** — Quarterly appraisal setup; goal setting; rating submission; review cycles
10. **Staff Timeline** — Chronological activity feed per staff member (attendance, leaves, tasks, reports, changes)
11. **Staff Availability** — Live presence status (Available, Busy, On Leave, In Meeting, etc.)
12. **Announcements** — Send announcements to departments, institutions, or specific roles
13. **Events Calendar** — Institution events with RSVP tracking
14. **Staff Recognition** — Kudos, Employee of the Month, birthday/work anniversary reminders
15. **Circulars & Document Repository** — Upload and manage HR policies, notices, forms
16. **Shift Management** — Define shifts; assign staff to shifts with effective dates and grace periods
17. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Create and analyze staff feedback
18. **Grievance Dashboard** — Anonymous/named feedback; response and resolution tracking
19. **Export & Compliance** — HR data export for audits and reporting
20. **Audit Log** — Complete action trail for HR actions

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for the Joint Director / HR team?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
