# ThaibaHive — Features for Group Of Schools (GOS)

Dear GOS Team,

ThaibaHive is being built as the unified digital workplace for all Thaiba Garden institutions. Below are the features planned that are relevant to your department. Please review and let us know if anything needs to be added, modified, or removed.

## Features

1. **Staff Directory (Multi-School)** — Full searchable directory of teachers and staff across all GOS schools; filters by school, subject, role
2. **Staff Management** — Add/edit teacher records; assign to multiple schools; track qualifications and assignments
3. **Attendance Management** — Teacher check-in/out across schools; late/early detection; school-wise reports
4. **Leave Management** — Digital apply→approve workflow; substitute planning; balance tracking
5. **Task Management** — Assign and track academic and administrative tasks
6. **Daily Reports** — End-of-day school activity summaries
7. **Announcements** — Broadcast to all GOS schools or specific campuses
8. **Events Calendar** — School events, parent meetings, exam schedules
9. **Circulars & Document Repository** — Store academic policies, curriculum docs, inspection reports
10. **Staff Polls & Surveys** — Teacher feedback and opinion collection
11. **Staff Recognition** — Teacher of the Month, kudos, work anniversaries
12. **Performance Reviews** — Quarterly teacher evaluations with goals and ratings
13. **Approval Center** — Approve/reject leave, purchase, and expense requests
14. **Staff Availability** — Live teacher availability status
15. **Grievances** — Staff feedback and suggestions
16. **Checklists (Onboarding/Offboarding)** — New teacher joining and exit formalities
17. **Shift Management** — Define school timing shifts; assign teachers
18. **Export** — Staff data export for regulatory reporting

## We Need Your Input

1. Are there any features listed above that should be **removed or modified** for GOS?
2. Are there any **additional features** you need that are not listed (e.g., class timetable management, student performance tracking, substitute teacher pool)?
3. Are there any **overall app features** you believe should be included for all departments?
