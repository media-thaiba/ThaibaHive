Executive Summary

ThaibaHive is not another HR application. It is the digital operating system for Thaiba Garden Group of Institutions—a unified workplace that replaces spreadsheets, paper registers, and fragmented WhatsApp communication with a single platform for attendance, tasks, leaves, reports, approvals, communication, resources, and analytics.

Built on the proven, production-tested foundation of MediaHive (already in daily use at the Media Department), ThaibaHive eliminates development risk and delivers a battle-tested platform at a fraction of the cost of commercial ERP or HRMS alternatives.

The approach is pilot-first and phased: a representative group of 30–50 staff across roles will validate each feature set in real conditions. After pilot success is confirmed against strict metrics, the full platform launches to all staff across all institutions simultaneously—maximizing adoption momentum while minimizing operational risk.

The ThaibaHive Platform

A unified system with 30+ integrated capabilities organized by function rather than priority — because every feature works together on one platform.

Workforce Management

Staff Attendance

NFC tag / QR code check-in & check-out. Configurable shift hours per department, institution, or individual. Late detection and real-time dashboard.

Leave Management

Digital apply → approve workflow. Leave type configuration, balance tracking, department-wise overview, auto-update on approval.

Task Management

Assign tasks to individuals or departments. Kanban board with To Do, In Progress, Review, Completed. Priorities, due dates, comments, file attachments.

Daily Work Reports

End-of-day summaries and task-based reporting. Configurable per staff role. Submission compliance tracking.

Staff Directory & Portfolio

Searchable directory with department, role, institution, contact information. Full HR profile: qualifications, certificates, experience, skills, languages, emergency contacts, Aadhaar/PAN, bank details, contract expiry.

Staff Timeline

Complete history per staff member — attendance, leaves, tasks, reports, promotions, department changes, warnings, appreciations — all in one chronological view.

Staff Availability

Live presence: Available, Busy, In Meeting, On Leave, Off Campus, Travelling. One-click status update visible across the platform.

Operations

Asset Management

QR-tagged institutional assets with purchase date, warranty, service history, assigned person. NFC scan for quick check-in/out.

Visitor Management

Reception registers visitors, scans ID, notifies host staff, prints visitor pass, records exit. Full audit trail for security.

Vehicle Management

Fleet registry, driver assignment, fuel logs, maintenance schedules, insurance expiry alerts, trip requests, fuel efficiency (km/L) calculation.

Canteen / Meal Management

Staff notify skip or guests per meal (breakfast/lunch/dinner). Headcount summary per meal type. Reduces food waste across campuses.

Resource Booking

Book auditoriums, cameras, projectors, conference halls, sports grounds. Live calendar avoids double booking.

IT Help Desk

Raise, track, and resolve IT support tickets with comment threads, priority levels, and assignment tracking.

Maintenance Tickets

Report facility issues (fan, leakage, electrical, internet). Assign technicians, track completion, SLA monitoring.

Communication & Engagement

Announcements & Notices

Broadcast to all staff, specific departments, or institutions. Read receipts and priority levels.

Events Calendar

Institution-wide and department events with RSVP, calendar views, and reminders.

Circulars & Document Repository

Central storage for policies, circulars, forms, and official documents. Searchable by category, date, department.

Staff Polls & Surveys

Quick feedback collection on proposals, event attendance, training needs. Live bar chart results.

Recognition & Badges

Employee of the Month, Excellent Teacher, Team Player, Innovation Award. Peer recognition tokens visible on profiles. Birthday & work anniversary auto-reminders.

Grievance / Suggestion Box

Anonymous or named submissions in categories: workplace, harassment, facilities, suggestion. Admin response flow with status tracking. Harassment complaints route directly to HR, never sit in an automated queue.

Internal Chat

Direct messages, department groups, file sharing, voice notes. Reduces dependency on personal WhatsApp groups.

Administration & Finance

Approval Center

Unified inbox for all approvals — leaves, purchases, expenses, IT requests, bookings, duty changes. Delegate approver support when managers are unavailable.

Expense Claims

Staff submit bills and travel expenses with photo upload. Approval workflow included. Track reimbursement status.

Purchase Requests

Multi-stage approval: Staff → HOD → Accounts → Purchase. Full audit trail and stage progress tracker.

Accounts & Financial Overview

Income/expense tracking per institution. Categories, transaction ledger with filters, summary dashboards for leadership.

Digital Forms Builder

Admins create custom forms without coding — hostel inspection, trip approval, campus inspection. Integrated approval workflows.

Executive Dashboard

Board-level view: staff present, leave statistics, task completion, department productivity, institution comparison, open tickets.

Intelligence & Automation

AI Assistant

Natural language queries: “Who is on leave tomorrow?”, “Show pending tasks for Media Department.” No menus required.

AI Daily Summary

Every morning: who is absent, important meetings, pending approvals, overdue tasks, birthdays, critical alerts. Delivered automatically.

AI-Assisted Report Generation

Generate monthly department reports, attendance summaries, and management meeting reports in one click.

Reminder Engine

Automatically reminds about expiring IDs, probation reviews, contract renewals, pending reports, unapproved leave, incomplete tasks.

Department Health Score

Each department scored on attendance, task completion, report submission, response time, leave trends. Identifies areas needing support.

Workflow Automation

Event-driven automation: when leave is approved → notify HOD → update attendance → inform security → update calendar.

  • Department Heads / HODs (5–8) — Validate task assignment, leave approval, team oversight

  • Support staff (8–12) — Security, housekeeping, transport — validate NFC/QR check-in
  • Media / IT Department (4–5) — Technical validation, feedback, bug reporting

Device Policy

Not all staff own smartphones capable of running the app. ThaibaHive addresses this with a tiered approach:

  • BYOD (Bring Your Own Device): Most staff use personal smartphones
  • Institution-provided tablets: Dedicated devices stationed at key entry points for QR/NFC check-in
  • NFC tags at turnstiles: Quick tap check-in without needing a phone
  • Paper fallback: Manual check-in option during the transition period
